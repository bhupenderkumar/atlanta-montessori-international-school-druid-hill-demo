Our Mission & History

Atlanta Montessori International School - Druid Hills was founded in 1994 with a vision of providing a high-quality, Montessori education to families in the Atlanta area. Our school has been serving the community for over 25 years, building a strong reputation for academic excellence and character development.

Our teaching approach is centered on the Montessori method, which emphasizes hands-on learning, self-directed exploration, and individualized instruction. This approach allows our students to develop a deep love of learning, a strong sense of self, and a commitment to community service.

In addition to our academic programs, we offer a range of extracurricular activities and character-building programs that foster social-emotional growth, creativity, and physical fitness. From arts and music to outdoor education and athletics, we provide our students with a rich and diverse experience that prepares them for success in all areas of life.
